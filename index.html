Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical Invisalign treatment take?

Most treatments range from 6 to 18 months, depending on the complexity of the case. During your consultation, Dr. Sharma will provide a personalized timeline.

Is the dental implant procedure painful?

We use advanced local anesthesia and sedation techniques to ensure you are completely comfortable. Post-procedure discomfort is minimal and easily managed with medication.

Do you offer emergency dental services?

Yes, we prioritize dental emergencies. Please call our clinic immediately if you are experiencing severe pain or have suffered a dental injury.
